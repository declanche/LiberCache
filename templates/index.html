What is LiberCache?

LiberCache is your personal library, designed to help you achieve your reading goals.

Our app allows you to:

  • Track your reading progress across multiple books
  • Maintain and organize your reading list
  • Set and monitor reading goals
Whether you're a casual reader or a dedicated bibliophile, LiberCache helps you stay organized, motivated, and connected to your reading journey. Start building your literary cache today!
